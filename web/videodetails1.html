Year

Description

"Pour Me A Drink" is a collaboration between Post Malone and Blake Shelton, featured on Post Malone’s sixth studio album F-1 Trillion, which marks his debut in the country genre.

The song is a lively anthem about unwinding after a tough week, showcasing a mix of themes from both artists: Post Malone's frustration with daily grind and Shelton's portrayal of romantic regret.

The music video enhances this mood with scenes of a spirited lakeside party that turns out to be part of Post Malone's dream sequence. Their chemistry and playful vibe are captured through lighthearted scenes, reflecting the song's feel-good nature.
