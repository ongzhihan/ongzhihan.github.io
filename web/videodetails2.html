I Had Some Help
ft. Morgan Wallen

Year

2024

Description

"I Had Some Help" by Post Malone featuring Morgan Wallen delves into the theme of toxic relationships, portraying how blame can be shared. The song emphasizes that both parties contribute to the breakdown, not just one.

The lyrics reflect on mutual faults, resonating with listeners who have experienced complicated romantic dynamics. Its country-infused sound features heartfelt, confessional lyrics balanced with a light, buddy-drinking anthem vibe, making it relatable yet catchy.

The song was teased by Wallen in late 2023 and was highlighted by performances such as their collaboration at Stagecoach 2024, which enhanced its popularity.
