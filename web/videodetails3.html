Chemical

Year

2024

Description

"Chemical" by Post Malone, released as the lead single from his fifth studio album in April 2023, delves into the emotional turmoil of navigating a toxic, on-and-off relationship.

The song reflects themes of dependency and the cyclical nature of such love, symbolized by the term "chemical" to highlight the uncontrollable, addictive nature of the bond.

Despite the longing for change, Post Malone acknowledges the pull of this connection as unavoidable, emphasizing his struggle to move on and his acceptance of these emotions.
